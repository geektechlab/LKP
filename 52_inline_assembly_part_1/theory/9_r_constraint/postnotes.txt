If r constraint is used, gcc may keep the value of 'val' in any register.

To specify the register, you must directly specify the register names by using specific register constraints

+---+--------------------+
| r |    Register(s)     |
+---+--------------------+
| a |   %eax, %ax, %al   |
| b |   %ebx, %bx, %bl   |
| c |   %ecx, %cx, %cl   |
| d |   %edx, %dx, %dl   |
| S |   %esi, %si        |
| D |   %edi, %di        |
+---+--------------------+
